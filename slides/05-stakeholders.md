## Stakeholders

# Who is affected?

- **Consumers**  
  Care about price, taste, health, convenience, habit, and social acceptance.

- **Farmers**  
  Need stable income, workable regulations, and respect for local conditions.

- **Animals**  
  Raise questions about welfare, living conditions, and the ethics of production.

- **Environment**  
  Connects meat production to land use, water, biodiversity, and emissions.

- **Restaurants**  
  Must balance customer demand, cost, staff skills, and menu identity.

- **Schools**  
  Can influence habits through meals, education, and discussion culture.
