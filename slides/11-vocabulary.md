## Vocabulary

# Difficult words

- **meat consumption**  
  how much meat people eat

- **to reduce**  
  to make something less

- **plant-based**  
  made from plants

- **carbon footprint**  
  climate impact

- **animal welfare**  
  how animals are treated

- **personal freedom**  
  the right to choose

- **sustainable**  
  good for the future

- **government regulation**  
  rules from the state

- **processed meat**  
  bacon, sausage or ham

- **balanced diet**  
  eating different healthy foods
