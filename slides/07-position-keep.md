## Position A

# Meat should remain normal

This side argues that meat can be part of a responsible lifestyle when choices are moderate and informed.

- **Culture:** Many meals and traditions are built around meat.
- **Nutrition:** Meat is a familiar source of protein, iron, and vitamin B12.
- **Economy:** Farmers, butchers, restaurants, and suppliers depend on the meat sector.
- **Freedom:** People may reject pressure or rules about personal food choices.
