## Context

# Why meat is a useful discussion topic

Meat sits at the intersection of personal habits, culture, health, economics, and climate.

- **Daily**  
  Food choices are repeated often, so small changes can become visible quickly.

- **Social**  
  Meals are tied to family, identity, celebrations, and tradition.

- **Debatable**  
  Good arguments exist on several sides, which makes it suitable for discussion practice.
