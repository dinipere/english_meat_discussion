## Introduction

# Why are we discussing meat?

- **51 kg**  
  Meat available per person/year in Switzerland

- **28%**  
  Food system share of Switzerland's total footprint

- **60 kg CO2e**  
  Climate impact of 1 kg beef

- **2-3x/week**  
  Swiss recommendation for meat maximum

<div class="footnote">Sources: Proviande 2025, BAFU, Our World in Data, FSVO</div>
