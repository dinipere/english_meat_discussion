## Central question

# The key tension

> **Should people reduce meat consumption, or should meat remain a normal part of everyday life?**
>
> The strongest discussion will not be "for or against meat" only. It will ask what kind of change is fair, realistic, and useful.

- **Individual freedom**  
  People want to decide what they eat without being judged.

- **Shared consequences**  
  Food systems affect land use, emissions, animal welfare, and public health.

- **Practical limits**  
  Price, access, cooking skills, and habits shape what people can actually change.
