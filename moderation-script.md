# Moderation Script

Use these sentences if you get stuck. Keep it simple and speak slowly.

## 00 Title

Hello everyone. We are Felix and Josia. Today we will moderate a class discussion about meat consumption and personal freedom.

## 01 Plan

This is not a normal presentation. We want you to speak. First we do a quick poll, then a position line, then partner talk and open discussion. At the end we vote again.

## 02 Rules

Please be respectful. Short answers are okay. Try to give one reason for your opinion.

## 03 Vocabulary

Before we start, we quickly explain some important words. Please ask if one word is unclear.

## 04 Warm-up Poll

Question one: How often do you eat meat in one week?

Question two: Should people in Switzerland eat less meat?

Question three: What word do you connect with meat?

Who wants to explain their answer in one sentence?

## 05 Context

This topic matters because meat is connected to climate, health, money, culture, animals and personal freedom.

## 06 Main Question

Our main question is: Should we eat less meat, or is that a matter of personal freedom?

## 07 Position Line

Please stand up. The left side means: we should eat less meat. The right side means: it is personal freedom. You can also stand in the middle.

Can one person from the left side explain why?

Can one person from the right side explain why?

## 08 Side A

Here are some reasons for eating less meat. Which reason is strongest for you?

## 09 Side B

Here are some reasons for personal freedom. When does advice become pressure?

## 10 Think - Pair - Share

Now please think alone for one minute. Write down one argument for each side. Then talk with your partner for two minutes.

Which pair wants to share one argument?

## 11 Open Discussion

Now we open the discussion. Please answer with "I think..." or "In my opinion..."

Let's hear another opinion.

Can you explain why?

Let's move on to the next question.

## 12 Lab-grown Meat

Now one technology question: If lab-grown meat tastes the same and is safe, would you eat it?

Why or why not?

## 13 English Help

You can also use these phrases during the discussion.

## 14 Final Vote

Did your opinion change? Please raise your hand for yes, no, or middle.

Thank you for the discussion. Our short summary is: there is no perfect answer. Eating less meat can help with climate, health and animal welfare, but food is also personal and connected to culture, money and freedom.
